E-Alert

An innovative mobile and web-based platform designed to enhance the management and monitoring of flood and road accident prone areas in Naga City. The application enables users to report real-time incidents related to flooding or road accidents. These reports are promptly shared with local residents and relevant authorities, fostering timely response and improved public safety. By leveraging technology, E-Alert aims to promote community resilience and efficient disaster management.
